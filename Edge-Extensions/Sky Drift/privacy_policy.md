# Privacy Policy for Sky Drift

**Last Updated:** June 4, 2026

## Introduction

Thank you for using Sky Drift. This Privacy Policy explains how Sky Drift handles user information.

Sky Drift is a browser-based arcade game designed to provide entertainment directly within the Chrome browser. We are committed to protecting user privacy and maintaining transparency regarding data practices.

## Information We Collect

Sky Drift does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information** from users.

The extension does not collect:

* Names
* Email addresses
* Phone numbers
* Postal addresses
* Payment information
* Authentication credentials
* Location data
* Browsing history
* Website content
* Personal communications
* Health information

## Local Data Storage

Sky Drift may store game-related information locally on the user's device, such as:

* High scores
* Game settings
* Gameplay preferences

This information remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external server.

## Data Sharing

Sky Drift does not sell, rent, transfer, or disclose user data to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Sky Drift does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or data collection services.

## Remote Code

Sky Drift does not load or execute remote code. All code required for operation is packaged within the extension distributed through the Chrome Web Store.

## Children's Privacy

Sky Drift does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

## Security

Because Sky Drift does not collect or transmit personal information, no personal user data is stored on external systems.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

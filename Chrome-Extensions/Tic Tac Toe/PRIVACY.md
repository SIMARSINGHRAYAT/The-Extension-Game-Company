# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-06

Thank you for using this browser extension (the "Extension"). Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension may collect, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## Summary

- **Data collected:** By default, the Extension collects no personal data and does not transmit browsing or personal information to servers.
- **Storage:** Any data stored by the Extension is stored locally in the browser (extension storage) unless you explicitly enable a feature that syncs data to a remote service.
- **Third parties:** The Extension does not share data with third parties, except for optional services you explicitly enable (see details below).

## Details

### 1. What we collect

- No personal information is collected by default. The Extension does not track browsing history, page content, form data, or other personal identifiers.
- If you opt into optional features (for example, cloud sync or crash reporting), you may choose to send limited data to third-party services. Those services and the data they collect will be listed in this policy and require your explicit consent.

### 2. How we use data

- Local-only features use data only within your browser to provide functionality (for example, storing preferences or game state in local extension storage).
- If you enable analytics or crash reporting, aggregated and anonymized usage metrics may be collected to help improve the Extension. This will never include personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

### 3. Cookies and tracking

- The Extension does not set cookies on websites. It may use the browser's extension storage (a scoped storage mechanism) to save preferences and local state.

### 4. Third-party services

- The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, cloud, or advertising services by default. If we later add optional integrations (e.g., Google Analytics, Sentry), those will be listed here and require your opt-in.

### 5. Data security

- Local data is stored using the browser's extension storage APIs. We do not transmit local storage data to remote servers unless you explicitly enable an optional upload/sync feature.
- While we take reasonable measures to protect data, no system is completely secure. Do not store sensitive personal information in the Extension unless explicitly supported and documented.

### 6. Your choices

- You can disable or remove the Extension at any time from your browser's Extensions page.
- If the Extension offers optional telemetry or sync features, you may disable them from the Extension's options/settings page.

### 7. Children

- The Extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 8. Changes to this policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the Effective Date above and, when feasible, notify users in the Extension changelog or via an update message.

If you have questions about this policy or the Extension's data practices, contact us at: your-email@example.com

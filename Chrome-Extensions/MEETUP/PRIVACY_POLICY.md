# Privacy Policy
**Meeting Slot Manager**
Last Updated: June 7, 2026

## Overview

Meeting Slot Manager is a browser extension designed to help users store and manage meeting links along with their selected date, time, and notes.  

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the extension collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

## Information We Collect

Meeting Slot Manager does **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data externally.

The extension only stores the information that you manually enter, which may include:

- Meeting title  
- Meeting URL  
- Selected date  
- Selected time  
- Notes or descriptions  

All of this information is stored **locally on your device** using the browser's storage API.

We do not have access to your data.

---

## How We Use Information

The information you enter is used solely to:

- Display your saved meetings inside the extension
- Allow you to edit or delete meetings
- Open saved meeting links in a new browser tab

No data is analyzed, shared, sold, or transmitted to any external server.

---

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally using the browser’s `storage` API.  

This means:

- Your data stays on your device  
- No external servers are used  
- The developer cannot access your information  

If you uninstall the extension, your stored data may be permanently deleted.

---

## Permissions Used

Meeting Slot Manager only uses the following permission:

### `storage`
This permission is required to save and retrieve your meeting data locally so that it persists between browser sessions.

No other sensitive permissions are requested.

---

## Remote Code

Meeting Slot Manager does not use remote code.

All scripts and functionality are packaged within the extension.  
No external JavaScript files, tracking scripts, analytics tools, or third-party services are used.

---

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use:

- Analytics services  
- Advertising networks  
- Tracking tools  
- External APIs  

The extension only opens meeting links in a new tab when you choose to do so.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not:

- Sell your data  
- Share your data  
- Transfer your data  
- Access your data  

Your information remains entirely under your control.

---

## Children's Privacy

Meeting Slot Manager does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the extension is safe for general audiences.
